How to get started
SafeChex is designed for a person to share their whereabouts and have an authorized user to retrieve that information. A valid phone number is needed to log your whereabouts and to retrieve your status. If you want to track your status go to new user check in. Update your status as it changes through the update previous check in. If you want to check on the status of someone, go to check on someone.
